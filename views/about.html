About

I have a deep interest in Computer Science and Software Development, and my objective is to make a positive impact on society by advancing the field of Information Technology. I find great satisfaction in tackling problems, embracing challenges, exploring new technologies, and crafting software applications for various platforms. I am receptive to strong viewpoints and encourage constructive feedback. Some of my hobbies are playing sports like badminton, watching movies and anime, reading articles about new technologies, appreciating music, and playing musical instruments such as the guitar and keyboard.
